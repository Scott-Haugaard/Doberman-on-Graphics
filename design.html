Spyder A chair with locomotion, that can be achieved by rocking or bouncing, so you crawl across the floor. The leg joints articulate, using springs and bumpers.

It bares the telltale hourglass .. Enjoy my pretties. Have fun !

Tree of Light A floor lamp comprised of gooseneck tubes, and LED tips. These can be focused from a flood light to narrow spot, in any color. The arms can be positioned in an infinite number of arrangements, while the lights provide even more drama.

The base is a weighted by a large semi-circle, providing am even balance, with a wide footprint.

Quake This table concept incorporates symmetry and chaos, by creating visual tension, and perceived motion. Weight with impossible lightness, as it appears to want to collapse under it's own weight, as gravity seems to shift, even in stillness.

The fun part is the ability to move the center slab into other positions, and to rotate the legs, creating different physicality.

Two inch Serpentine slabs, on 1.25" bent chromed legs. About 20" wide, by hip-high. Background inspired by the Frank Lloyd Wright's Ennis house.

Warptrobe Doors and Drawers both share the same hinges.

Enough capacity to comfortably host a single person. Everything to dress is right in front of you. The vanity mirrors show every angle. Ouch!

Room for shoes on one side would eliminate the need of a joint. Future iteration could have asymmetrical hinges, based on the depth of drawers and voids.

SPCAmbulance A quick-response vehicle for animal rescue. The rear compartment can accommodate a large bear, or two.

The low center of gravity, wide stance, and high clearance make it stable through rough terrain.

Medical supplies are accessible from the interior, and pullout drawers on the outside. Oversized equipment is stored below, across the chassis, or on the roof.

Neptone A jukebox that can load two disks simultaneously, so segues can be created between songs.

Disks are mounted face down, with a system of LEDs ringed around the disks' mirrored surfaces. Providing a light show, projecting from all sides.
